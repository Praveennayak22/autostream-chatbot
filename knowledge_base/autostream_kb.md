# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is an AI-powered SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators — YouTubers, Instagram creators, TikTokers, and other social media professionals. It helps you edit videos faster and smarter with AI.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan — $29/month
- 10 videos per month
- 720p resolution export
- Automated cuts and transitions
- Basic color correction
- Email support (business hours only)
- Up to 30 minutes per video

### Pro Plan — $79/month
- Unlimited videos per month
- 4K resolution export
- AI captions (auto-generated subtitles)
- Advanced color grading
- AI-powered scene detection
- Priority 24/7 customer support
- Custom branding and watermark removal
- Up to 4 hours per video

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?**
Yes! You can upgrade at any time. The price difference is prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

**Do I need technical skills?**
No! AutoStream is designed for creators, not engineers. The interface is simple, intuitive, and requires no technical background.

**How long does video processing take?**
Most videos are processed within 2–5 minutes depending on length and resolution.

**Is there a limit on video length?**
Basic Plan: up to 30 minutes per video. Pro Plan: up to 4 hours per video.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

**What makes AutoStream different from manual editing?**
AutoStream uses AI to cut editing time by up to 80%. Instead of spending hours editing, you upload your footage and let AI handle the heavy lifting — cuts, transitions, captions, and color grading — in minutes.
